The following questions are about where you recreated in Mid-Atlantic coastal and ocean areas (see Map) during the last 12 months.

The Mid-Atlantic coastal and ocean areas include the states of New York, New Jersey, Delaware, Maryland, and Virginia. For the purposes of the survey, recreation includes activities such as beach going, wildlife viewing, scenic enjoyment, surfing, kayaking, etc. This survey is not concerned with consumptive activities such as fishing or clamming.
